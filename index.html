The Delicacy of Chocolate.

What is chocolate?

Chocolate is a food made from roasted and grounded cocoa beans. It can be liquid, solid or paste. It can be by itself or it can flavor other foods. It is the most famous food.

History of chocolate.

Chocolate dates back more than 5000 years when the cacao tree was first domesticated in present-day Mexico. Early consumption dates to 600 BC. This delicacy evolved as far as being a drink.

Types of chocolates

Chocolates have 3 variations and with a change in concetration you can have a different types of chocolate.

  • Dark
  • Milk
  • White

Flavors of chocolate

Mint is the only flavor of chocolate that exists. It can be peppermint, spearmint or mint cream. This is done by adding it as a flavoring to the chocolate.
